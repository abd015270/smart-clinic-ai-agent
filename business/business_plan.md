\# Smart Clinic AI Agent - Business Plan



\## 1. Problem



Small and medium clinics waste many hours every week answering repeated patient questions, checking appointment availability, managing bookings, and handling urgent requests manually.



Patients also face difficulty finding the right clinic, choosing the correct specialty, and knowing available appointment times quickly.



\## 2. Solution



Smart Clinic AI Agent is an AI-powered clinic assistant that helps patients find suitable clinics, check available doctors, book appointments, ask clinic-related questions, and send emergency alerts.



The system is not just a chatbot. It is a real AI Agent with:



\* Role

\* Goal

\* Backstory

\* Tools

\* RAG knowledge base

\* Skills file

\* Evaluation tests



The agent can search clinic data, check doctor availability, create appointment bookings, generate booking codes, retrieve policy answers from local knowledge files, and register emergency alerts.



\## 3. Target Audience



The target customers are small and medium medical clinics that need an affordable digital assistant to reduce administrative workload.



Main target segments:



\* Dental clinics

\* Family medicine clinics

\* Pediatric clinics

\* Dermatology clinics

\* Cardiology clinics

\* Small clinic groups with multiple doctors



The first version focuses on clinics in Palestinian cities such as Nablus, Hebron, Ramallah, Jenin, and Bethlehem.



\## 4. Revenue Model



The project uses a monthly subscription model.



Suggested pricing:



\* Basic Plan: $29 per clinic per month

\* Pro Plan: $59 per clinic per month

\* Enterprise Plan: custom pricing for clinic groups



The Basic Plan includes appointment booking, FAQ answers, and dashboard access.



The Pro Plan adds emergency alerts, advanced reports, and more clinic customization.



\## 5. Market and Competitors



There are many clinic management systems, but many of them are expensive, complex, or focused only on internal clinic management.



Smart Clinic AI Agent is different because it focuses on patient interaction before the visit:



\* Finding the right clinic

\* Answering repeated questions

\* Booking appointments

\* Generating booking codes

\* Sending emergency alerts

\* Reducing receptionist workload



The main advantage is that it is simple, affordable, and designed for small and medium clinics.



\## 6. Go-To-Market Strategy



To get the first 10 customers, the team will:



1\. Contact local clinics directly.

2\. Offer a free 14-day trial.

3\. Demonstrate how the agent books appointments in under one minute.

4\. Show the dashboard with appointments and emergency alerts.

5\. Focus first on dental clinics because booking appointments is frequent and repeated.

6\. Ask early clinics for feedback and testimonials.

7\. Create short demo videos for WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram.

8\. Offer a discounted first-month subscription.

9\. Partner with local developers or digital marketing agencies.

10\. Use referrals from the first clinics to reach more clinics.



\## 7. Costs



Estimated monthly operating cost for 1,000 patient interactions:



\* AI token cost: about $3

\* Server hosting: about $10

\* Database cost: $0 in the demo version

\* Total estimated cost: about $13 per month



Suggested subscription price:



\* $29 per clinic per month



This gives a good profit margin while keeping the product affordable for small clinics.



\## 8. Product Features



Current demo features:



\* AI Agent using CrewAI

\* Clinic search by city and specialty

\* Doctor availability checking

\* Appointment booking

\* Booking code generation

\* Emergency alert registration

\* RAG knowledge base for FAQ and policies

\* Skills file for specialized clinic assistant behavior

\* Evals with 5 test cases

\* Streamlit live demo

\* Clinic dashboard



\## 9. Competitive Advantage



Smart Clinic AI Agent has three main advantages:



1\. It is specialized for clinics, not a general AI chatbot.

2\. It performs real actions using tools, not only text responses.

3\. It combines RAG and Evals, which makes the system more reliable and measurable.
