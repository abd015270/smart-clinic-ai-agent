\# Booking Policy



\## Appointment booking

Patients can book appointments by selecting their city, medical specialty, preferred clinic, doctor, and available time slot.



\## Booking confirmation

After booking, the system generates a unique booking code. The booking status becomes confirmed.



\## Late arrival

If a patient arrives more than 15 minutes late, the clinic may delay the appointment or ask the patient to book another time.



\## Cancellation

Patients should cancel appointments at least 24 hours before the appointment time.



\## Modification

Patients can request to modify the appointment time if another available slot exists.



\## Emergency priority

Emergency cases have priority over normal appointments when the clinic supports emergency handling.



\## Patient information

The system stores basic appointment information such as patient name, phone number, city, specialty, clinic, doctor, appointment slot, and booking code.
