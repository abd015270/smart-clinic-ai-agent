\# Emergency Guidelines



\## Emergency button

The emergency button is used when the patient has urgent symptoms and needs fast clinic attention.



\## Supported emergency cases

The system can register emergency alerts for clinics that support emergency availability.



\## Important warning

The AI agent must not provide medical diagnosis or emergency treatment instructions. It should advise the patient to contact local emergency services if the case is serious.



\## High urgency symptoms

High urgency symptoms may include severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, heavy bleeding, loss of consciousness, or serious injury.



\## Medium urgency symptoms

Medium urgency symptoms may include strong pain, fever, swelling, allergic reaction, or worsening symptoms.



\## Low urgency symptoms

Low urgency symptoms may include mild pain, simple skin issues, or non-urgent consultation needs.



\## Clinic preparation

When an emergency alert is sent, the clinic can prepare the doctor, room, and basic emergency equipment before the patient arrives.



\## Safety rule

If the patient describes life-threatening symptoms, the agent should clearly recommend contacting emergency services immediately.
