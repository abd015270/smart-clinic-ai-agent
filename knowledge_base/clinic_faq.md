\# Smart Clinic FAQ



\## What is Smart Clinic AI Agent?

Smart Clinic AI Agent is an intelligent assistant that helps patients find clinics, choose specialties, book appointments, and ask clinic-related questions.



\## What specialties are available?

The current system supports Dentistry, Family Medicine, Pediatrics, Cardiology, Dermatology, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, ENT, Gynecology, and Neurology.



\## What cities are supported?

The current system supports Tel Aviv, Jerusalem, Haifa, Beersheba, Nazareth, Eilat, Netanya, Ashdod, Rishon LeZion, Petah Tikva, Holon, Herzliya, Acre, Tiberias, and Safed.



\## Can the patient book an appointment?

Yes. The patient can choose a city, specialty, clinic, doctor, and available time slot.



\## Can the patient cancel or modify an appointment?

In this demo version, the patient can ask about cancellation and modification rules. Full cancellation and modification can be added as a future feature.



\## Does the system replace a doctor?

No. The system does not replace doctors. It only helps with clinic selection, booking, and administrative support.



\## Can the system answer medical diagnosis questions?

No. The system should not provide medical diagnosis. It can only give general guidance and recommend contacting a doctor or emergency service when needed.



\## What should the patient bring to the appointment?

The patient should bring a personal ID, phone number, previous medical reports if available, and the booking code generated by the system.



\## What is the booking code?

The booking code is a unique number given to the patient after confirming an appointment. The patient shows it at the clinic reception.



\## Are the clinics in this project real?

The clinics and doctors in this demo are fictional sample data created for the final project demonstration.
